October · Infant

The Great Outdoors · a note to take home

Autumn is a feast for the senses. Bundle up and let baby feel the wind, hear the birds, and touch a leaf — that’s real learning.

Letters T · G · JNumber 3Shape: triangleColor: brownTheme: The Great Outdoors

Try this at home

  • Go on a “listen for the birds” walk.
  • Let baby touch a smooth and a crinkly leaf (watch closely).
  • Make the /t/ “tick-tock” clock sound.
  • Point to the sun, a tree, a bird.

Sing together

  • “The Leaves Are Falling”
  • “Twinkle, Twinkle”

Read together

Cuddle up and read See Me Eat Meat in a soft voice. It’s printed in this packet, and free on Bear Phonics.
