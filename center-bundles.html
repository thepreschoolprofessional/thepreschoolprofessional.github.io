Block Center

Toddler – VPK

Early math, physics, and teamwork disguised as play. A well-stocked block center keeps builders busy for a full center rotation.

  • Wooden unit blocks (classroom set)
  • Cardboard brick blocks
  • Toy vehicle set
  • Wooden train set
  • Block play people (diverse)
  • Traffic signs
  • Storage shelf with bins

How to use these

Three steps to a finished center.

1. Pick your center. Each bundle above is complete on its own — click through the items, compare options and prices, and choose what fits your space and budget.

2. Check the ages. Open the Milestone Wizard, pick your state and age band, and you'll see exactly which skills each center supports — plus the specific materials each age group needs.

3. Set it up with purpose. Shelf height at children's eye level, materials they can reach themselves, and a plan to rotate. A center children can use independently is a center that teaches all day.
